What is in a breakfast taco?

Well, breakfast tacos can have whatever you'd like in them! I like to make ones for the morning savory but feel free to put whatever you'd like in them. When I originally made these, I was focusing on my macros.

How do you make breakfast tacos?

Do you prefer flour or corn tortillas? The base sets the mood. Below I will list what was in the ones pictured above:

  1. Choose your base: always corn for me.
  2. Add your proteins: I scrambled eggs and pan fried turkey bacon.
  3. Add your toppings: I chose avocado for a filling fatty fiber source.
  4. Sauce it up if you'd like: I added Cholula sauce! Yum!

Ingredients Used

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Organic Brown Eggs
  • Turkey Bacon
  • Avocado
  • Cholula Hot Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
