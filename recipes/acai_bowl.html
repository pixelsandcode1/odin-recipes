What is an acai bowl?

Magic in a bowl. Really. A fun way to start the morning with pureed Brazilian acai berry with fun texture toppings.

What is in an acai bowl?

This is another subjective question, as are most recipes! In my acai bowl I like:

  • Acai
  • Blueberries
  • High Protein Granola
  • Raspberries
  • Honey
  • Almond Milk

Unpictured

  • Banana
  • Shaved Almonds
  • Unsweetened Coconut Shavings

How do you make an acai bowl?

Forewarning, I use a ninja creami to make these and they turn out sooo good this way! But a blender will do!

  1. In a creami cup, place your acai balls and blueberries. Freeze for 24 hours.
  2. Run the creami once on smoothie bowl.
  3. Add a splash of plant-based milk to the creami cup, and re-spin.
  4. Once at the desired consistency, scoop into a bowl.
  5. Add fruit, granola, honey, and other toppings!
  6. Enjoy your delicious bowl.
